# DigitalMarketingStrategyReportforWeAreParadoxx-

 ### [YouTube Demonstration](https://youtu.be/7eJexJVCqJo)

<h2>Description</h2>
I developed the digital marketing plan for WE ARE PARADOXX, an eco-luxury hair care brand, to enhance product exposure, attract eco-conscious customers, and strengthen its market position. I utilized the RACE framework to map the customer journey and create detailed buyer personas, setting ambitious goals: a 15% increase in revenue, a 10% boost in sentiment and engagement, and a 20% growth in followers. Early results showed promising follower growth and engagement, though high bounce rates impacted conversion goals. To address this, I recommend allocating more time for research to optimize checkout process, invest in advanced analytics training, integrating AI for predictive analytics and personalized marketing, thus making iterative plan updates.
